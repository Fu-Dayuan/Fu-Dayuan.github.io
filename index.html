Biography

I am a final-year Master student of Pattern Recognition and Intelligent System Laboratory in the Department of Artificial Intelligence, Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications(BUPT), advised by Prof. Weiran Xu. Previously, I received my B.S. degree from the Department of Information and Communication Engineering, Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications in July, 2021.

Currently, My research interests focus on Information Extration, Information Retrieval and Large Language Modeling.
